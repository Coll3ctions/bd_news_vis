turning a blind eye
to the casual observer, it might seem that bird flu is no longer news. it rarely appears in the papers any more, and one could be forgiven for thinking that the massive damage it did to the poultry industry a few years ago was a thing of the past. but this is not true. the outbreak of bird flu at a bio-secure breeder farm owned by kazi farms in north bengal, a region that had been considered safe, shows that the threat is not over. rather, it seems to be increasing, invading locations and well-managed farms that were previously not affected.
