wake up women, stand against cervical cancer
cervical cancer which affects lower third of uterus (also called cervix) is the most common cancer in women in bangladesh. it constitutes about one fourth of the total number of female cancers patient in bangladesh. an estimated about 13,000 new cases and 6600 deaths occur every year in bangladesh. most of these deaths are preventable if they would be detected early.
