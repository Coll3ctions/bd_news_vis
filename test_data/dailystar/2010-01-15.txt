fiscal year

two weeks into the new year and tech enthusiasts are already sniffing around for a whiff of what to look out for in 2010. technology progresses so fast, it is sometimes just too tough to keep up. you buy the latest 3g mobile phone and are all set to show it off to your friends when your ego just comes crashing down as you find that it's already passé and that 4g mobile phones are the new best thing in the market. even then technology can be such an enticer, the rushed progress doesn't stop many from pining for the latest gadget on the block, even if it means that your pockets are not going to like the sound of that.
