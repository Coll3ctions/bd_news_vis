the puzzle of soil fertility
solids, liquids, and gases are three forms of matter in soil. solids constitute soil reserves of most nutrients, provide physical support for plants and provide a means of holding added nutrients in the root zone. soil water is the immediate source of most nutrients absorbed by plant roots. soil gases occupy the soil pores with water.
