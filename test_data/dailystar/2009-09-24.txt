challenges galore in bangladesh
it was a feeling of humiliation that gave brick and mortar to the people in east pakistan to build a country of their own. that was some 38 years ago when bangladesh was born. with skimpy resources and a larger population, it did not flag their determination to convert the country into a sonar bangla. but gradually a mood of uncertainty had taken over the nation.
