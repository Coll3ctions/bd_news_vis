population problem: forewarning of bigger ones
an oversized population is a problem for any country irrespective of its stage of development. the problem is really complex for a developing country because of resource constraint. we economists at times downplay the problem and argue that developed manpower, whatever its size, will always augment welfare of the country. the argument is built on economic indices: gdp, per capita income, foreign exchange reserve, productivity etc.
