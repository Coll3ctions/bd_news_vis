connectivity the key
never since 1975, the year of catastrophic change in bangladesh's political leadership, has indo-bangladesh relationship struck such a cordial cord as it is apparent today. the relationship was warmed through prime minister sheikh hasina's visit to delhi, and has engendered prospects in the area of infrastructural connectivity between the neighbours. however, this august beginning has not gone without comment from the opposition political leadership and policy pundits.
