strongman in trouble
president parvez musharraf, once pakistan's powerful general who banished two elected prime ministers, is going to be the first to face impeachment in pakistan's sixty-one year history. the matter has ignited both attention and tension in pakistan, in the region, and in the world. the us state department reacted cautiously to the move. pakistan's ruling coalition stated that it was an internal matter. "we have consistently said it is the internal politics of pakistan and an issue for the pakistani people to decide," said state department spokesperson gonzago galegoes, pointing out that washington and islamabad remain close allies in the war against terror.
