guantanamo trials should be open
recently, i stood before a military judge at guantanamo bay to argue that the press and public had a constitutional right to observe the proceedings of military commissions. it is an argument i've made scores of times on behalf of news organisations objecting to closed proceedings in criminal and civil trials, but this was the first time that a military commission -- part of a system of tribunals created in 2006 to try terrorism suspects -- agreed to hear such arguments from the press.
